## Suggested Pitfalls (for §4)

1. **Confusing an AI demo with a real business.** Per the channel's philosophy, AI is leverage on existing advantage — not a replacement.
2. **Building before validating.** Test the niche first.
3. **Spending big on infrastructure.** Cheap stack first. Upgrade only when revenue justifies.
4. **Ignoring compliance.** Privacy, data retention, terms of service matter.
5. **Skipping the legal basics.** Business entity, taxes, contracts.

## Glossary Seeds

- **Stack** — the combination of tools used to run a business.
- **AI agent team** — multiple AI agents working together on different parts of a business.
- **MVP (Minimum Viable Product)** — the simplest version of a product that delivers value.
- **Compliance** — legal and regulatory requirements (privacy, tax, data handling).
- **Moat** — a sustainable competitive advantage.
