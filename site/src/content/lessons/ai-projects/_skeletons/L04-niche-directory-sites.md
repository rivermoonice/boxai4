## Suggested Pitfalls (for §4)

1. **Starting without a niche advantage.** Per the channel: "best directories succeed because the founder picked a niche where they had personal advantage." AI can't manufacture this.
2. **Expecting $5K/month in 30 days.** Per the channel, "best built with cheap no-code + LLM stacks." Realistic timeline: 6–18 months.
3. **Spending $5K on the build.** Cheap stack exists. Use Hostinger + Kimi.
4. **Building for ads before traffic.** AdSense revenue requires organic traffic. Paid listings come first.
5. **Quitting after 30 days.** Per the channel: "directories generate $5K–$100K/month via AdSense, best when founder has personal niche advantage."

## Glossary Seeds

- **Niche directory** — a curated website listing businesses or resources in a specific vertical.
- **Niche advantage** — personal expertise, network, or knowledge in a specific vertical that gives you an edge.
- **Hostinger Horizons** — a no-code platform the channel uses for cheap builds.
- **AdSense** — Google's ad network. The secondary monetization for directories.
- **Paid listings** — featured business listings on a directory. The primary monetization per the channel.
- **Moat** — a sustainable competitive advantage.
