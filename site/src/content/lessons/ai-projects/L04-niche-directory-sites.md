### Step 1 — The honest business model

A niche directory is a curated website that lists businesses, resources, or content in a specific vertical. Examples:
- A directory of AI coding tools.
- A directory of crypto orderflow platforms.
- A directory of remote work tools for designers.

The business model is straightforward:
1. Build the directory (one-time cost).
2. Get traffic (organic SEO, paid listings).
3. Monetise (paid listings, then AdSense).

**The source video's actual case study:** The channel built a directory in 48 hours using AI. The build was fast. The traffic and monetisation take much longer.

### Step 2 — The prerequisites most beginners don't have

Per `Specs/ChannelIdentity.md`: "Best directories succeed because the founder picked a niche where they had personal advantage."

Three prerequisites:

1. **Niche advantage.** You know the vertical better than 90% of people. You're not learning the niche — you're organising what you already know.
2. **Network.** You know people in the niche. They'll provide listings, share your directory, give feedback.
3. **Time.** 6–18 months to first meaningful income. Most beginners quit before this.

If you don't have all three, this project will fail. Not because of the AI tools — because of the prerequisites.

> Source: `_8iv521cy_A` — Per the channel's documented belief, the founder's prior niche advantage is the real moat. AI amplifies the moat; it doesn't create it.

### Step 3 — The Hostinger + Kimi stack

The channel's documented stack:

- **Hostinger Horizons** — no-code platform for the build. Cheap, fast, drag-and-drop.
- **Kimi K2.7** — the model for content generation. Cheap, good for UI/vision.
- **Hermes (optional)** — for ongoing automation (auto-update listings, scheduled content).

**Why this stack:**
- **Cost.** Hostinger Horizons is $3–$10/mo. Kimi is $10–$20/mo. Total: under $30/mo to start.
- **Speed.** Vibe coding on Kimi generates content fast.
- **Maintenance.** Hermes (if you go that route) handles ongoing updates.

> Source: `_8iv521cy_A` — "Built with cheap no-code + LLM stacks like Hostinger Horizons + Kimi."

### Step 4 — The 90-day launch plan

**Days 1–7: Niche validation.**
- Write down 50 businesses/resources in your niche from memory.
- If you can't, you don't have niche advantage. Pick a different project.
- Survey the niche: what directories already exist? What's missing?

**Days 8–14: Build the MVP.**
- Sign up for Hostinger Horizons.
- Build a directory with 30 listings (vibe-coded).
- Set up Kimi API for content generation.

**Days 15–30: Initial traffic.**
- Submit to relevant communities (Reddit, Twitter, Discord, niche forums).
- Reach out to your network for backlinks.
- Don't pay for ads yet.

**Days 30–60: Iterate.**
- Add more listings (target 100 by day 60).
- Improve SEO based on what's working.
- Reach out to listings for paid placements (the channel's primary monetisation).

**Days 60–90: Monetise.**
- Convert at least 10 listings to paid placements.
- Set up AdSense as secondary monetisation.
- Track traffic, conversion, revenue.

### Step 5 — Realistic income expectations

The channel's documented belief: "Directories generate $5K–$100K/month via AdSense, best when founder has personal niche advantage, Hostinger Horizons + Kimi is the default toolstack for non-technical builders, and paid featured listings come before AdSense in the monetization sequence."

**Realistic ranges:**
- **Months 1–6:** $0–$500/mo (mostly from a few paid listings).
- **Months 6–12:** $500–$5K/mo (more listings, organic traffic growing).
- **Months 12+:** $5K–$100K/mo (if niche is good and execution is solid).

The top end requires real niche advantage, sustained execution, and usually some scale (multiple directories or adjacent products).

## 4. Common Pitfalls & Troubleshooting

### Pitfall 1 — Starting without a niche advantage

- **Symptom:** You pick a niche you don't know. You can't list 50 businesses from memory.
- **Cause:** AI hype made you think you can build a directory in any niche.
- **Fix:** Pick a niche you know deeply. If you don't have one, build something else.

### Pitfall 2 — Expecting $5K in 30 days

- **Symptom:** You build the directory and wait for income. It doesn't come.
- **Cause:** Traffic and conversion take 6–12 months minimum.
- **Fix:** Set realistic expectations. Build for the long term.

### Pitfall 3 — Spending $5K on the build

- **Symptom:** You buy a custom theme, hire a designer, pay for premium hosting.
- **Cause:** Treating a hobby project like a funded startup.
- **Fix:** Cheap stack first. Hostinger Horizons + Kimi. Upgrade only when revenue justifies.

### Pitfall 4 — Building for ads before traffic

- **Symptom:** You optimise for AdSense before you have visitors.
- **Cause:** Beginners conflate "monetisation" with "AdSense."
- **Fix:** Paid listings first, AdSense second. Per the channel: "Paid featured listings come before AdSense in the monetization sequence."

### Pitfall 5 — Quitting after 30 days

- **Symptom:** You give up before the 6-month mark.
- **Cause:** No traffic, no income, frustration.
- **Fix:** Set realistic expectations upfront. 6–18 months to meaningful income.

### Pitfall 6 — Treating AI as the product

- **Symptom:** You spend weeks on the AI prompt instead of getting listings.
- **Cause:** Building the AI feels productive. Listing businesses doesn't.
- **Fix:** Listings are the product. The AI is the tool. Get 100 listings first, then refine.

### Pitfall 7 — Building for "AI can do it" not "people need it"

- **Symptom:** Your directory exists but no one visits.
- **Cause:** You built it because AI could, not because there was demand.
- **Fix:** Validate demand before building. Survey 20 people in the niche first.

### Pitfall 8 — Ignoring SEO

- **Symptom:** Your directory has no organic traffic after 6 months.
- **Cause:** No SEO work.
- **Fix:** Each listing should be a proper page with unique content. SEO is the long-term traffic engine.

## 9. FAQ

**Q: How much can a niche directory make?**
A: $5K–$100K/month per the channel's documented belief. Most successful directories are in the $5K–$20K range.

**Q: How long until I make my first dollar?**
A: Realistically 3–6 months for the first paid listing. 6–18 months for meaningful income.

**Q: Do I need a niche advantage to start?**
A: Yes. Per the channel: "Best directories succeed because the founder picked a niche where they had personal advantage." If you don't have one, pick a different project.

**Q: Can I build a directory without AI?**
A: Yes. AI just makes it faster. The fundamentals (niche advantage, listings, traffic) are the same.

**Q: What if my niche is too small?**
A: Smaller niches have less competition. If you can list 50 businesses, the niche is large enough. If not, it's too small.

**Q: Should I build for AdSense from day one?**
A: No. Per the channel: "Paid featured listings come before AdSense in the monetization sequence." AdSense requires organic traffic, which takes months to build.

**Q: Will AI build my directory for me?**
A: The build, yes. The traffic and monetisation, no. Those require human network and persistence.

**Q: What if I have niche expertise but no network?**
A: The network builds over time as you operate the directory. Start anyway. Reach out to people in the niche. Invite listings. Build the network.
